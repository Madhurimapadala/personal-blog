FloraWeb

Flora Web is an innovative website project, serving as a comprehensive online repository for technical plant care information, guidance, and community engagement. Its mission is to empower individuals, from novice gardeners to seasoned horticulturists, to effectively care for their plants through a robust database of detailed species profiles. Users can access essential growth and care information, fostering knowledge-sharing and support within a plant care community. Beyond plant care, the platform promotes environmental awareness, educating users on sustainable horticultural practices.
